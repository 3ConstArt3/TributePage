Time table

1452

Leonardo’s Birth at Anchiano, two miles from Vinci.

1473

Leonardo makes Landscape Drawing, his oldest surviving drawing.

1488

First Anatomical drawings appear in his notebooks.

1494

May have painted Virgin of the Rocks; construction of canal for statue.

1498

Leonardo plans to publish his notebooks; writes book on theory of mechanics.

1505

Finishes full-size sketch of Battle painting; does many nature sketches.

1466

Moves to Florence to apprentice in Verrocchio’s workshop.

1480

Leonardo works for Duke Lorenzo de Medici, a patron of the arts.

1490

Leonardo directs feasts and pageants; considers making a telescope.

1495

Begins Last Supper in the dining room at the Santa Maria delle Grazie.

1503

Commissioned to paint Battle of Anghiari; begins Mona Lisa.

1507

Brief trip to Florence; makes plans to publish his notebooks.
